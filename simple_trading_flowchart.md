## Key Points for Non-Technical Users:

✅ **Always Running**: If AI is down, backup system takes over  
✅ **Customizable Risk**: You choose how conservative or aggressive to be  
✅ **Smart Analysis**: System studies market patterns before deciding  
✅ **Safety First**: Multiple protections to minimize losses  
✅ **Real-Time Data**: Uses current market information  

## What You See:

- **Dashboard**: Shows your portfolio value changing over time
- **Decisions**: BUY, SELL, or HOLD displayed for each asset
- **Performance**: How much profit or loss you're making
- **Risk Profile**: Shows which risk level you selected
